List of Assumptions (Last Modified 00:07 10/19/2015)
====================================================
1. All GeoSeek Users have Android smartphones capable with the
minimum requirements (running Android 4.4). All Android smartphones
used by GeoSeek Users have cameras.
2. GeoSeek Users have smartphones that can capture Photo(s) during
GeoSeek gameplay.
3. Some GeoSeek Users will play anonymously (non-Guild) play. All 
GeoSeek Users capable of anonymous play are able to joing a GeoSeek Guild.
4. GeoSeek Users can join more than one Guild.
5. Users can only play in one GameSession at a time.
6. Guild vs. Guild is not supported. Guild statistics are tracked based on
the results of GeoSeek Users that compete in a GameSession.
